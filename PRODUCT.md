# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Football fans following the 2026 World Cup who keep Khoros open as a small companion window (460×760) NEXT to a match stream or their work. They glance, type a quick take, share a photo, peek at the live room, and go back to the match. Sessions are short and frequent; the window is never their primary focus. The real evaluating audience is the Tether/QVAC team judging the Developers Cup: they read the interface as evidence the QVAC SDK's on-device primitives (LLM, STT, TTS, diffusion, OCR, fine-tune) compose into one coherent product.

## Product Purpose

A society of AI agents that lives entirely on the user's device (QVAC SDK) and experiences the World Cup with them: a personal agent with persistent memory, live match rooms fed by real ESPN data, a lounge where agents banter, and cross-device agent meetups over a thin E2E-encrypted relay. Success = every on-device primitive demonstrably working inside one app that feels alive, private, and honest.

## Brand Personality

Stadium-night broadcast: warm gold on charcoal, floodlit, a little theatrical at match moments. Precise and honest everywhere else. Three words: electric, trustworthy, compact.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS chatbot shells (white sidebar + bubble list + fat toolbars).
- Dashboard-cliché: hero metrics, identical card grids, gradient text.
- Anything that overclaims: no "zero-cloud" copy, no fake data, no cosmetic features. If it renders, it must be real.

## Design Principles

- Content over chrome: in a 460×760 window every persistent bar must pay rent. Rarely-used controls collapse behind an affordance; the chat thread and the match feed get the space.
- Broadcast moments, quiet base: goals, callbacks and evolutions may flare (confetti, glow); everything between stays calm so the flare lands.
- One glance, one truth: state (live / upcoming / replay, on-device, transcribing) is always visible and never misleading.
- Show the primitive: each QVAC capability surfaces as a natural product gesture (talk, listen, read, imagine, evolve), not as a settings toggle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: text contrast ≥4.5:1 (both themes), visible keyboard focus, hit targets ≥32px, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected for the celebratory animations, never color-only state signaling (badges carry text).
